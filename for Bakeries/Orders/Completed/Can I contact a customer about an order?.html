Of course! There are a few ways that you can reach out to customers.

There are 3 ways you can reach out to customers: Order notes, Phone, Email. All of this information is easily found on the Orders Page

Order Notes

We have a feature called Order Notes. Order notes is a super fast way to email a message to your customer.

How to send an Order Note

  1. Start at your Orders page.
  2. Click the Order Notes link on the customer's order.
  3. You will get a popup where you can enter in your message.
  4. When you are done, click Add order note.

You will be able to see a record of the Order Note by clicking on the Order Notes link again.

Phone

Customers are required to enter a phone number when they checkout. You can use this if you need to quickly contact a customer.

Email

Customers are also required to have an active email. Keep in mind we can't see any back and forth communication on outside emails.
