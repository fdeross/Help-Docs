Simple Subscription

This is the same as a Simple Product, but the customer will be automatically be charged at intervals that you set for a period of time that you set. You can think of it like a Netflix, Birchbox, or Dollar Shave Club but for your bakery.

Variable Subscription

A Variable Subscription allows you to set different options for the customer to choose from. Let's say you primarily bake bread and you want to offer several subscription options: Twice Weekly, Weekly, and Every Other Week. You could set these as variations under one Variable Subscription.

Add a Subscription

  1. Go to the Shop Manager > Products and click Add Product.

  2. Fill out the product information for your item.

  3. Select either Simple Subscription or Variable Subscription under Product Type.

üóí Note

There will be new options under the General tab for your Subscription settings.

  1. Set the terms for the subscription, including price, billing interval, billing period, length, sign-up fee and trial period (sign-up fee and trial period are optional).

Learn more: Subscription Options

üí° Tip

We will tell the customer during checkout how often the subscription will charge, but you will probably want to include that information on the Short Description as well.

  1. Once you have the product information filled out, click Save Changes at the bottom of the page.

Edit an existing Subscription Product

  1. Go to the Shop Manager > Products.
  2. Find the Subscription you want to edit.
  3. Click Edit on the product listing.
  4. When you are done with your changes, click Save Changes at the bottom of the page.

Common Questions

Simple Subscription vs Variable Subscription

The Variable Subscription product type is similar to Variable Product.

In addition to allowing different product attributes, each variation in a Variable Subscription can have a unique:

  • Sign-up fee
  • Trial period
  • Recurring price
  • Billing period
  • Subscription length
