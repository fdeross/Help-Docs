Easily connect your Frosting shop to Stripe and start accepting payments.

Connect to Stripe

  1. From the Shop Manager go to the Settings page.

  1. Click on the Payments tab.

  1. Click the "Connect with Stripe" button.

  1. You will be taken to Stripe to create or to log in to an existing account. Stripe will automatically connect to your Frosting account and you will be ready to take your first order!

Disconnect your Stripe account

Click on the Disconnect button on the Payments tab.
